The Impact and Use of Colors in Living Spaces

We spend 80% of our lives indoors. The interiors where we live and spend our time have a great influence on our mood with their design. The colors of living spaces also affect our psychology and each color adds a different atmosphere to the space. Designing the spaces we live in to feel good makes the time we spend in these interiors much more enjoyable and productive.

Use of colors in Interior

Use of Color in Interiors Colors greatly affect how we feel in the space we live in. In addition, the color and pattern choices we use in our living spaces reflect our personality. We can think of your floor and wall floors as the canvases of our living spaces. By reflecting our ideas and creativity on these floors, we can transform our living spaces into our own work of art.

The use of bold colors and patterns allows your interior design to emerge. The use of colored, patterned porcelain and ceramic tiles creates an invigorating effect while creating a high-energy atmosphere in the interior.

Colors help people to separate spaces and find direction in the buildings they are in. Separating the space transitions according to colors, patterns and tones will make the interior more readable.

Color and Pattern in Porcelain and Ceramic Tiles

With the developing technology, we can use unique colors and patterns in ceramic and porcelain tile design. With digital printing, we can reflect our creative ideas on ceramic and porcelain tiles. As Rino | Seramik Porselen, we are excited to create original products by combining traditional craftsmanship and modern technology.

Blue

Blue color is effective in creating a sense of confidence with its deep and bold tones. Blue has a calming and serene energy. The use of blue color is ideal for areas where you want to create a relaxing environment. You can use blue tones on walls and floors in areas where you expect people to feel peaceful, such as bedrooms, living rooms and waiting areas. Green

The color green brings to mind natural life, plants and positive concepts, promoting peace and relaxation. You can apply the green color in areas where you want to create a natural atmosphere. Nebula Porcelain Tile inspired by nature and precious stones: https://rino.com.tr/en/products/nebula

Dark and Light Tones

Dark tones allow you to add depth to interiors. Colors such as dark blue, gray and anthracite create a consistent and modern atmosphere in living spaces with minimal choices that emerge when used with light tones such as white, cream and wooden materials.
